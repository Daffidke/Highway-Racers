Bárkányi Dávid X28GSU

Goal of The Game

  • Two cars are racing on the highway.
  • As they go faster and faster, they need to avoid the cars in front of them.
  • Drive and avoid the obstacles on the highway.
  • After 5 crashes, one of the cars' engine will fail, so they stop.
  • You can choose a difficulty by selecting the size of the highway.
  • A score boost and a speed change happens every 10 seconds.
  • Get more and more points during the process.
  • Get on top of the leaderboard by getting the best highscore.
  • Creator: Bárkányi Dávid, X28GSU

Controls

  • Player One (Red Car) - Arrows
  • Player Two (Blue Car) - WASD
  • Enable or Disable music by clicking the button in the menu.
  • By default, it plays during the game.
  • Enable or Disable SFX by clicking the button in the menu.
  • By default, it's enabled during the game.
